1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY & INVESTMENT THESIS
================================================================================

1.1 THE VISION
--------------
We are building the definitive "Professional Operating System" for Bangladesh and emerging markets. Smart Contact Wallet transforms the archaic practice of exchanging paper business cards into an intelligent, always-updated digital network that serves as the foundation for professional relationships and, eventually, B2B commerce.

1.2 THE CORE INNOVATION
-----------------------
Unlike traditional contact apps that simply digitize static information, Smart Contact Wallet introduces three breakthrough features:

**A. Living Network Intelligence**
Every contact is connected to a "Global Directory" - an anonymized source of truth. When a contact updates their job, phone number, or company, YOUR address book updates automatically. This eliminates the "dead number" problem that plagues every professional.

**B. AI-Powered Trust Layer**
Our system analyzes patterns to detect fraudulent contacts and spam numbers. Users see "Verified" badges for legitimate professionals and "Community Alerts" for suspicious numbers - creating a self-governing trust network.

**C. Contextual Business Tools**
From the contact card, users can instantly: Call, Email, WhatsApp, Pay (via integrated wallet), View on Map, or Share their own digital card via NFC/QR. Every interaction is logged, creating a rich history of professional relationships.

-----------------------------------
**Meet Rahim**, a 32-year-old sales manager at a textile company in Dhaka.

**Day 1: Networking Event**
- Rahim attends a trade fair. He meets 15 potential clients.
- Each person hands him a paper business card.
- Rahim shoves them in his wallet.

**Day 3: Post-Event Follow-Up**

- He manually types 11 contacts into his phone. Takes 45 minutes.

**Month 6: The Dead End**
- Rahim needs to call Mr. Ahmed (met at event). 
- The number is disconnected. Mr. Ahmed changed jobs and got a new company phone.
- Rahim has no way to find him. **Connection lost forever**.

4. OUR SOLUTION: INTELLIGENT CONTACT ECOSYSTEM
================================================================================

4.1 PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY
----------------------
**Principle #1: Offline-First**
The app must work perfectly even without internet. All data is stored locally (Isar database) and syncs when online. This ensures reliability in Bangladesh's variable connectivity environment.

**Principle #2: Intelligence, Not Just Storage**
We don't just "save" contacts. We enrich, verify, update, and analyze them to provide actionable intelligence.

**Principle #3: Zero Friction**
Every feature is designed for one-handed use, minimal taps, and instant gratification. Scanning a card takes 2 seconds. Sharing your info takes 1 tap.

4.2 CORE FEATURE BREAKDOWN (60+ SCREENS)
-----------------------------------------

**MODULE 1: INTELLIGENT SCANNING (5 Screens)**

Screen 1-2: Camera Scanner (Front & Back)
- **Technical**: Real-time card detection with alignment guides
- **UX**: Visual rectangle overlay shows user exactly where to position card
- **AI**: OCR extracts Name, Title, Company, Phone, Email, Address with 92% accuracy
- **Innovation**: Dual capture (front AND back) preserves all information including logos

Screen 3: OCR Confirmation & Edit
- **Smart Fields**: System highlights low-confidence extractions in yellow for manual verification
- **Auto-Formatting**: Phone numbers auto-convert to +880 Bangladesh format
- **Duplicate Detection**: Instant check against existing contacts, prompts merge if found

**MODULE 2: THE LIVING NETWORK (Global Directory)**

How It Works:
1. **Hashing & Privacy**: When you scan Mr. Ahmed's card, we hash his phone number (SHA-256) and create a "Global Contact" entry
2. **Decentralized Update**: When Mr. Ahmed creates his own "My Card" and updates his info, the Global Entry updates
3. **Smart Notification**: You receive a toast: "Mr. Ahmed updated his profile. New position: Director at XYZ Corp. Update?"
4. **User Control**: You choose to accept, ignore, or merge the new data

**Privacy Guarantee**: Your personal notes, tags, and private fields NEVER sync to the Global Directory. Only verified, public professional information is shared.

**MODULE 3: MY CARD CREATOR (Professional Identity)**

Screen: Card Design Studio
- **Customization Options**:
  - **Templates**: 12 pre-designed layouts (Minimal, Corporate, Creative, Modern)
  - **Logo Upload**: Drag-and-drop company logo with size/position controls
  - **Color Themes**: Brand color picker (select from pre-sets or custom hex)
  - **Custom Fields**: Add specialized info (e.g., "Portfolio Link", "WhatsApp Business")
  
- **Dynamic QR Code**: 
  - Generates unique QR linked to your cloud profile
  - If you update your details, the QR automatically reflects changes (no reprinting needed)
  - Scannable by ANY camera app - no app install required for recipient

**MODULE 4: FRICTIONLESS SHARING (NFC + QR)**

**NFC "Bump" Exchange** (Unique Feature):
- **Flow**: Open app → Tap "Share" → Physically tap phones together → Cards exchanged instantly
- **Technical**: Uses Android Beam / iOS Core NFC for peer-to-peer transfer
- **Visual**: Ripple animation radiates from center of screen with haptic feedback
- **Speed**: 0.5 seconds from tap to save

**Alternative Methods**:
- **QR Code**: Full-screen scannable code with download link
- **vCard Export**: Standard .vcf file for compatibility with any phone
- **PDF Version**: Generates printable digital business card
- **Share Link**: Web link opens your digital profile (no app required)

**MODULE 5: INTERNAL WALLET (V1: Bookkeeping)**

Dashboard Components:
- **Balance Display**: Shows current internal wallet balance in BDT
- **Quick Actions**: 
  - "Add Money" (Demo: simulates adding funds)
  - "Send to Contact" (Select from contacts, logs transaction internally)
  - "Request Payment" (Generates payment QR)
  
- **Transaction History**: 
  - Filterable by: Type (Sent/Received), Date Range, Category
  - Shows: Amount, Counterparty, Timestamp, Status

**Important Note (V1 Scope)**: This is an INTERNAL ledger only. No real money moves. This establishes the UI/UX foundation for V2 integration with bKash/Nagad.

**MODULE 6: TEAM COLLABORATION (Shared Directories)**

Use Case: A construction company with 5 project managers.

Setup:
1. Team Admin creates "ABC Construction Team"
2. Invites 5 members via email/QR
3. Designates "Shared Directory"

Workflow:
- PM #1 scans a supplier's card at a site visit
- Card automatically appears in "Team Directory"
- PM #2 can now call that supplier directly without asking PM #1
- **Business Value**: Company retains knowledge even if PM #1 leaves

**MODULE 7: ADVANCED SEARCH & FILTERS**

Search Capabilities:
- **Text Search**: Name, Company, Job Title, Phone, Email (instant results)
- **Visual Filter Bar**: 
  - Category Pills: [All] [Business] [Personal] [VIP] [Suppliers]
  - Sort Options: Recently Added | Alphabetical | Most Contacted
- **Advanced Filters Modal**:
  - Date Added (Last Week, Last Month, Custom Range)
  - Location (City, Country)
  - Tags (Multi-select)
  - MFS Status (Has bKash | Has Nagad | Unknown)

**MODULE 8: SETTINGS & SECURITY (12 Screens)**

Key Features:
1. **Biometric Lock**: Fingerprint/FaceID required to open app
2. **Backup & Sync**: 
   - Automatic cloud backup to Azure Blob Storage
   - Manual export to local ZIP file (includes all images + JSON)
3. **Privacy Controls**:
   - "Hide Balance" toggle for wallet
   - "Opt-in to Global Directory" (user decides if their "My Card" is searchable)
4. **Appearance**: Light/Dark mode with system preference auto-detection
5. **Category Management**: Create custom categories with color coding

6.2 OFFLINE-FIRST ARCHITECTURE (RELIABILITY MOAT)
--------------------------------------------------
**The Problem with Competitors**:
Most apps (LinkedIn, CamCard) are cloud-dependent. Poor network = unusable app.

**Our Solution: Isar Local Database**
- **What It Is**: Local NoSQL database storing ALL user data on-device
- **Performance**: 100,000+ reads per second (faster than querying a server)
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption at rest, protects sensitive data even if device is compromised

**User Experience Benefit**:
- App opens instantly (no "loading" spinner waiting for server)
- All features work on airplane mode
- Changes sync automatically when connection returns
- **Trust Factor**: "Your data is ON your phone, not just in the cloud"

**Technical Example**:
```
User Action: Save Contact
  Step 1: Write to Isar (local) - 5ms ✓
  Step 2: Show success to user - instant feedback
  Step 3: Queue sync job
  Step 4: When online, push to Azure Cosmos - happens in background
```
